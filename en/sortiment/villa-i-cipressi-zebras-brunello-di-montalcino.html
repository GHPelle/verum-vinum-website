Zebras Brunello di Montalcino

Villa i Cipressi

Region

Montalcino, Toscana, Italien

Grapes

100% Brunello (Sangiovese)

About the wine

The top wine – made from the very best grapes of the vineyards. Concentrated and complex with enormous ageing potential.

Alcohol

14%

Soil

Kalkhaltig, lera & sandig silt

Altitude

250-450 m ö.h.

Vintages

2015, 2016

Farming

Organic

Serving temperature

ca 18°C

Vinification

Hand-harvested, manual selection before pressing. Maceration and fermentation for 20-25 days in temperature-controlled stainless steel tanks (26/28°C). The wine is aged for 30-36 months, 60% in French oak barrique (225L) and the remaining 40% in oak casks (10/30hL), followed by at least 18 months in bottle.

Wine profile

Intense bright ruby red colour. Intense bouquet with great complexity, hints of red berries, violet, mild spices, with a touch of tobacco and vanilla. Good structure and soft tannins, balanced with a long finish.

Pairs with

Excellent with grilled meat, game, stews and hard cheese or simply as a meditation wine.

Ratings

James Suckling 96p/95p, Robert Parker 92p, Wine Enthusiast 93p
