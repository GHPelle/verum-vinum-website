Sparkling

Gougères – Franska ostpuffar

Classic French cheese puffs with gruyère, crispy on the outside and soft inside. Perfect snack with a glass of cold Champagne.

Show recipe
Ingredients

1 dl water, 1 dl milk, 100g butter, pinch of salt, 1.5 dl flour, 4 eggs, 150g grated gruyère, black pepper, nutmeg

Preparation

Oven 200°C. Boil water, milk, butter and salt. Stir in flour, work into a dough. Cool 5 min, stir in eggs one at a time. Mix in cheese and spices. Pipe onto baking sheet. Bake 20–25 min.

Wine pairing

The Champagne's bubbles and acidity cut through the buttery dough and melting gruyère. The saltiness enhances the wine's fruit.

Crostini med räkor, citron och dill

Crispy bread slices topped with sautéed prawns, fresh lemon and dill. The Mediterranean meets Scandinavia.

Show recipe
Ingredients

Sourdough baguette, 300g prawns, butter, olive oil, garlic, lemon, dill, crème fraîche, flaky salt

Preparation

Toast bread in oven 200°C. Fry prawns in butter 2 min. Add garlic. Deglaze with lemon juice, fold in dill. Spread crème fraîche on bread, top with prawns and dill.

Wine pairing

Dry Champagne and shellfish is timeless. Minerality and acidity mirror the prawns' salinity, the bubbles lift the flavours.

Stekt potatis med gräddfil och forellrom

Crispy fried potatoes with cold sour cream and glistening trout roe – luxurious everyday food that's ridiculously simple.

Show recipe
Ingredients

800g waxy potatoes (boiled), 3 tbsp butter, olive oil, flaky salt, 2 dl sour cream, 80g trout roe, chives

Preparation

Cut potatoes in halves. Fry in butter 5–7 min per side until golden. Salt with flaky salt. Top with sour cream and trout roe. Garnish with chives.

Wine pairing

Salt, fat and bubbles – the magic triangle. The trout roe's umami makes the wine's fruit sing.

Rosé

For dry, elegant rosés

Ratatouille à la rustique

Rustic version of the Provençal classic with large chunks of summer vegetables and tomato sauce.

Show recipe
Ingredients

Aubergine, zucchini, peppers, onion, garlic, crushed tomatoes, olive oil, tomato purée, thyme, basil, red wine vinegar

Preparation

Oven 220°C. Roast vegetables 25 min. Sauté onion, garlic, tomato purée. Add tomatoes and thyme, simmer 10 min. Fold in roasted vegetables. Top with basil.

Wine pairing

Rosé and ratatouille share the same home – Provence. The wine's berry notes and acidity meet the tomato's sweetness perfectly.

Bruschetta med tomat, burrata och pesto

Grilled sourdough bread with sun-golden tomatoes, creamy burrata and homemade basil pesto.

Show recipe
Ingredients

Sourdough bread, ripe tomatoes, burrata, basil, pine nuts, parmesan, garlic, olive oil, flaky salt

Preparation

Blend pesto from basil, pine nuts, parmesan, garlic and olive oil. Grill bread, rub with garlic. Spread pesto, top with tomatoes and burrata. Drizzle olive oil.

Wine pairing

The rosé's acidity meets the tomatoes' sweetness, the burrata's creaminess is softened by the wine's light body.

Grillad halloumi med vattenmelon och mynta

Fresh salad with golden grilled halloumi, sweet watermelon and cooling mint.

Show recipe
Ingredients

Halloumi, watermelon, red onion, olives, mint, rocket, olive oil, lime, honey

Preparation

Whisk dressing of olive oil, lime and honey. Grill halloumi 2 min/side. Lay out rocket, watermelon, halloumi. Scatter olives and mint. Drizzle dressing.

Wine pairing

The rosé's coolness mirrors the salad's freshness, the wine's sweetness harmonises with the watermelon.

Chardonnay – Bourgogne-stil

Ugnsbakad kyckling med dragon och crème fraîche

French everyday dish with chicken legs, tarragon and crème fraîche. The flavours develop beautifully in the oven.

Show recipe
Ingredients

4 chicken legs, crème fraîche, Dijon mustard, tarragon, garlic, butter, white wine, lemon

Preparation

Oven 200°C. Brown chicken in butter. Mix crème fraîche, mustard, garlic, tarragon and lemon. Pour wine in the pan. Spread mixture over chicken. Bake 35–40 min.

Wine pairing

The creaminess creates a perfect bridge to Burgundy Chardonnay's texture. The tarragon's anise flavour lifts the wine's minerality.

Pilgrimsmusslor med brynt smör och hasselnötter

Large scallops seared in brown butter, topped with roasted hazelnuts and lemon. Under 15 minutes.

Show recipe
Ingredients

8 scallops, 60g butter, hazelnuts, capers, lemon, parsley

Preparation

Pat scallops dry, season. Sear in butter 2 min/side. Remove. Brown butter with hazelnuts. Add capers. Lemon juice and parsley. Spoon over scallops.

Wine pairing

The scallops' sweetness and brown butter's nutty character is classic with Burgundy Chardonnay. The hazelnuts mirror the oak character.

Ingredients

4 trout fillets, butter, capers, lemon, parsley, salt, pepper, flour

Sauerkraut med fläskkorv och senap

Classic German home cooking – sauerkraut with juniper and caraway, coarse pork sausage and sharp mustard.

Show recipe
Ingredients

Sauerkraut, 4 pork sausages, onion, goose fat, white wine, juniper berries, caraway, bay leaves, potatoes, mustard

Preparation

Sauté onion in goose fat. Add sauerkraut, juniper, caraway, bay leaves and wine. Simmer 30 min. Place sausages on top, 15–20 min. Serve with potatoes and mustard.

Wine pairing

The sauerkraut's acidity and the juniper's spiciness is classic with Mosel Riesling. The wine's acidity cuts through the sausage fat.

Sparris med skirat smör och hackade ägg

Green or white asparagus with clarified butter and chopped boiled eggs. Simpler and better is not needed.

Show recipe
Ingredients

500g asparagus, 100g butter, 3 eggs (hard-boiled), chives, parsley, flaky salt, lemon

Preparation

Boil asparagus al dente. Melt and clarify the butter. Roughly chop the eggs. Place asparagus on platter, drizzle clarified butter. Scatter eggs, chives, parsley. Lemon juice.

Wine pairing

Dry Riesling is one of few wines that works with asparagus. Acidity and minerality balance the green notes.

Nebbiolo – Piemonte-stil (Barolo/Barbaresco)

Brasato al Barolo – Långbrässerad oxbringa

Piedmontese classic – beef brisket that melds with Barolo wine during several hours of braising.

Show recipe
Ingredients

1.2kg beef brisket, 1 bottle Nebbiolo, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon, tomato purée, olive oil, butter

Preparation

Marinate brisket in wine with vegetables and spices overnight. Brown the meat. Sauté vegetables. Add tomato purée and marinade. Oven 150°C for 3–3.5 hours. Blend sauce, slice meat. Serve with polenta.

Wine pairing

Barolo's powerful tannins demand rich, flavourful meat. The wine's roses and tar meld with the wine-based sauce.

Risotto al Tartufo – Tryffelrisotto

Creamy risotto with truffle – simple at its core but extraordinary in flavour. Piedmontese tradition.

Show recipe
Ingredients

320g risotto rice, chicken broth, onion, white wine, Parmigiano, butter, olive oil, black truffle or truffle oil

Preparation

Sauté onion in butter/oil. Toast rice. Pour in wine. Add broth ladle by ladle for 16–18 min. Remove from heat, stir in butter and parmesan. Shave truffle on top.

Wine pairing

The truffle's earthy aroma is one of the most classic pairings with Nebbiolo. Alba is the centre of both Barolo and white truffles.

Tajarin med korvragù – Piemontesisk äggpasta

Piedmont's own egg pasta with rustic ragù of Italian sausage, rosemary and red wine.

Show recipe
Ingredients

Tipo 00 flour, egg yolks, salsiccia, onion, carrot, celery, garlic, red wine, crushed tomatoes, rosemary, Parmigiano

Preparation

Make dough from flour and egg yolks, rest 30 min. Roll thin, cut into narrow strips. Brown crumbled sausage. Sauté vegetables. Wine, tomatoes, rosemary – simmer 30 min. Cook pasta 2–3 min. Toss with ragù and parmesan.

Wine pairing

The egg-rich pasta's buttery character pairs with Nebbiolo's complex tannins. The ragù's spicy flavour echoes the wine's notes.

Ankbröst med körsbärssås

Crispy duck breast with glossy cherry sauce – elegant yet surprisingly simple.

Show recipe
Ingredients

2 duck breasts, cherries, broth, Pinot Noir, honey, red wine vinegar, shallot, thyme, butter

Preparation

Score skin side. Place skin-down in cold pan 12–15 min. Flip, cook 3–4 min. Rest 10 min. Make sauce: sauté shallot, wine, cherries, broth, honey, vinegar. Cook 8–10 min. Slice duck, drizzle sauce.

Wine pairing

Pinot Noir and duck – a love story. The cherry sauce echoes the wine's notes, the acidity cuts through the duck fat.

Svamptärta med timjan och gruyère

Rustic open pie with wild mushrooms, gruyère and thyme. Perfect as a lighter dinner.

Show recipe
Ingredients

Pastry (flour, butter, egg yolk, water), mixed mushrooms, gruyère, eggs, cream, shallots, garlic, thyme, nutmeg

Preparation

Make pastry, chill 30 min. Line tin, blind bake 190°C 15 min. Sauté mushrooms in batches. Fry shallots, garlic, thyme. Whisk eggs-cream. Cheese in shell, mushrooms on top, pour custard. Bake 180°C 25–30 min.

Wine pairing

Mushrooms and Pinot Noir share the same earthy aroma profile. The gruyère's nuttiness and pastry's butteriness meet the wine's body.

Baga – Bairrada, Portugal

Leitão à Bairrada – Kryddig fläskstek (hemmaversion)

Portugal's most iconic meat dish – pork with crispy skin and spicy pimentón marinade.

Show recipe
Ingredients

1.5kg pork shoulder with rind, garlic, pimentón, black pepper, salt, bay leaves, chilli flakes, white wine, olive oil, lemon

Preparation

Day before: make spice paste of garlic, pimentón, pepper, salt, bay leaves, wine, olive oil, lemon. Score the rind. Rub meat side, refrigerate overnight. Oven 230°C 30 min, then 160°C 1.5–2 hours. Rest, slice.

Wine pairing

Baga's powerful tannins demand rich, fatty food – suckling pig is the historic regional pairing.

Show recipe
Ingredients

600g pork in cubes, clams, garlic, paprika paste/pimentón, white wine, vinegar, bay leaves, paprika, olive oil, potatoes, coriander
